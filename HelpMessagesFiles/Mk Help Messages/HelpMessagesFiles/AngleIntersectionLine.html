Angle Intersection Line

  • Click on Line Parameter and draw two Intersection lines on the image to draw Angle intersection Line.

  • Refer Line Parameter.

  • After drawing the Intersection lines, click on intersection point in the property window.


  • Select AngleIntersectionLine.

  • Click on intersecting lines one after another and you will get an Angle Intersection Line as shown.

  • Right click on the image to save the parameter.
