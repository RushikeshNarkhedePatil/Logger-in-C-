Parallelity

  • Go to Special Parameters and click on Geometric Dimensions.

  • Click on Geometric Dimensions and select Parallelity Parameter.

  • Select two lines one after another. If no line present, draw two lines with the help of Line parameter.

  • Line Parameter.


  • After properly clicking on two lines, you will find a tolerence window. Enter the values in tolerence window and click on save button to save the parameter.

  • Likely, you will get a Parallelity.
