Circle Centre


  • Once you click on Circle Parameter, right click on the image and draw a circle parameter where user wants to draw a circle.

  • Refer Circle Parameter.

  • While applying circle on the image, inner and outer layer need to apply as shown in figure.

  • If parameter not showing green circle between inner and outer layer, adjust the properties of circle.

  • Once you find the green circle, right click on it and you will get blue colour circle on image. As shown in above figure.







  • After drawing a circle, Select intersection point. Inside intersection point click on Circle centre.

  • Click on the edge of drawn circle. You will see a yellow cross in the centre of the circle. Likely you will get the Circle centre. As shown in the below figure.
