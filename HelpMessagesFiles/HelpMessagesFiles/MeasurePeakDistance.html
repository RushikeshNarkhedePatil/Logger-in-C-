Measure Peak Distance:

  • Click on Special Parameters.

  • Click on Advanced dimensions inside Specific Dimensions.

  • Click on MeasurePeakDistance.


  • Draw a rectangle on image where you want to Measure Peak Distance.

  • Right click on image to save changes.

  • Enter Valid data in Parameter Name of Tolerance Window.

  • Click on save button.

  • After clicking save button you will get a Measure Peak Distance.
