Measure Peak Distance From Base:

  • Click on Special Parameters.

  • Click on Advanced dimensions inside Specific Dimensions.

  • Select MeasurePeakDistanceFromBase.



  • Draw a line or step line at base of image where you want to Measure Peak Distance From Base.


  • Draw a rectangle and see where you getting the peak for image. Right click on image to save changes.

  • You will get Peak Distance From Base.

  • Enter Valid data in Parameter Name of Tolerance Window.

  • Click on save button.

  • After clicking save button you will get a Measure Peak Distance From Base.
