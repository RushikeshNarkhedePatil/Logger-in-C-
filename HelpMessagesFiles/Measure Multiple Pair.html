Measure Multiple Pair

  • Go to Special Parameters. Click on Specific Dimensions. Select Measure Multiple Pair.

  • Draw a rectangle on image where you want a Multiple Measure Pair. Adjust the Lines as per requirement.








  • Check advanced parameters and input parameters. Use Edge Difference as well as Tolerence properties for adjusting the Lines.

  • Once you adjust the lines, right click on image to save the region.

  • Now, select the Yellow Lines, to measure the distance between them.

  • The selelcted lines will appear green and selecting them after one another will show the distance.

  • Enter Valid data in Tolerance Window.

  • Again Follow the same procedure to measure the possible distances between two lines and enter valid data in Tolerence Window.

  • Once you get all distances click on Finish Button to complete the operation.
