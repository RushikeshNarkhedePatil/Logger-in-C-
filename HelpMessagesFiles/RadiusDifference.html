Radius Difference:

  • Draw two circles on the image with the help of Circle parameter.

  • Refer Circle Parameter.

  • After drawing two circles, go to property window, click on dimensions and select radius difference.





  • Select two circles and click them one after another.

  • After properly clicking on two circles, you will find a tolerence window. Enter the values in tolerence window and click on save button to save the parameter.

  • Likely, you will find a Radius Difference.
